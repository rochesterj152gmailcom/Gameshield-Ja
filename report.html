What to Do Next

1

Secure your account immediately

Change your password and enable MFA on the affected account. Do this before doing anything else.

2

Check linked accounts

Check your email and any other accounts that share the same password. Change those too.

3

Report to the platform

Use the official reporting or support feature on the affected platform. Most gaming platforms have a dedicated security or account recovery process.

4

Alert your contacts

If attackers used your account to contact others, let your gaming friends know so they don't fall for follow-up scams.
