Incident Reporting

Report a Cyber Incident

Encountered a scam, phishing attempt, account breach, or suspicious activity while gaming online in Jamaica? Use this form to document and report it.

Your report helps identify patterns and informs awareness efforts for the wider Jamaican gaming community.

What to Do Next

1

Secure your account immediately

Change your password and enable MFA on the affected account. Do this before doing anything else.

2

Check linked accounts

Check your email and any other accounts that share the same password. Change those too.

3

Report to the platform

Use the official reporting or support feature on the affected platform. Most gaming platforms have a dedicated security or account recovery process.

4

Alert your contacts

If attackers used your account to contact others, let your gaming friends know so they don't fall for follow-up scams.

5

Report to the JCF Cybercrime Division

For financial loss, identity theft, or serious account breaches, escalate to the Jamaica Constabulary Force Cybercrime Division. This is an official channel recommended by our research for Jamaican gamers facing significant cyber incidents.
